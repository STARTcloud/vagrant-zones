Vagrant-Zone is a plugin for Vagrant which will allow you to sping up bhyve, kvm, LX, illumos, and other zone types as you would a VirtualBox vagrant machine.

Manage with Confidence

Versioning, and automated deployment controls allow you to build your Virtual Machines with confidence the process will be transparent.

Create your Automation

The ability to quickly spin up zones of any type in a repeatable fashion allows, portable, versioned and automatable packages ensuring your projects environments are uniform and debuggable.

Share with Others

This tool will allow you to package and share boxes with other users, helping you develope on a wide array of Hypervisors and Operating Systems.
